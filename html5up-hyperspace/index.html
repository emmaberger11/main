Emma Berger

I'm a 4th year PhD student in Mike Crommie's experimental physics research group at UC Berkeley. I do research on scanning tunneling microscopy electron spin resonance (STM-ESR), which allows me to detect and control quantum states at the atomic scale. Broadly speaking, I'm interested in quantum computing, nanoscience, and condensed matter physics.
